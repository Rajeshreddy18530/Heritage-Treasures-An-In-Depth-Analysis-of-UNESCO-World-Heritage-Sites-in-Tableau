Heritage Treasures

An In-Depth Analysis of UNESCO World Heritage Sites

About the Project

This project explores UNESCO World Heritage Sites through the lens of data. Using Tableau, we bring the data to life with interactive visuals that uncover patterns, highlight endangered sites, and help understand the global heritage landscape more deeply. Our goal is to raise awareness and offer an engaging learning experience about our world's cultural and natural treasures.

Key Features

  • Interactive visualizations using Tableau
  • Country-wise and continent-wise breakdown
  • Insights into endangered sites
  • Category-wise comparison: Cultural, Natural, and Mixed
